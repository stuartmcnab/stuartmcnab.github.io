  3. JavaScript Drum Kit Demo

This is a demonstration of my JavaScript skills, linking keyboard inputs to generate a virtual drum kit as part of the JavaScript30 challenge. To make a sound, press a key on you keyboard and the corresponding sound will play.

If you are on a mobile, or touchscreen device you can simply touch the letter on your screen (or click with a mouse); Giving additional functionality outside of the original spec.
A clap
S hi hat
D kick
F open hat
G boom
H ride
J snare
K tom
L tink
