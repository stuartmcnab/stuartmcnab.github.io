Privacy

Respecting privacy

I believe that privacy is fundamental to life, and that includes the internet. This site is hosted on github's pages service and can be seen publicly, code and all. I don't want this website to collect your data, find out who you are, or advertise to you; so I don't use tracking cookies or pixels on this site.

Find out more about how some companies may track you on the internet from the Open Rights Group and the Electronic Frontier Foundation
